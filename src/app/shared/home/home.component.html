Welcome to your Engagement Portal

Designed to bring together CSC's partners, stakeholders and volunteers for ongoing dialogue, consultation and collaboration, the EP will enable to to connect with an on-line community of like-minded individuals and organizations - a way to engage with it's stakeholders

Engagement and Communications

The EP will serve asa a modernized stakeholder repository, while acting as a collaboration space to support, sustain and manage ongoing community engagement initiatives. A few features of the RP:

  • A centralized, searchable database of advisory groups, CSC partners and stakeholders
  • The ability to collaborate and consult on documents with partners and stakeholders remotely on various projects and tasks
  • Interaction, including e-communications, questionnaires/analytics
  • A document repository of resource material, including training and orientation
  • Online discussion forums

As a member, you can:

  • Register yourself or your organization as a stakeholders
  • Access a ceteralized, searchable database of CSC partners and stakeholders
  • Search out community engagement events
  • Collaborate with CSC and other stakeholders
  • Take part in the online discussion forums
  • Apply for volunteer positions
