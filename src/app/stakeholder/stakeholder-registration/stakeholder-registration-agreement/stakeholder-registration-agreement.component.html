Privacy Notice

The information you provide is to support Correctional Service Canada (CSC) in providing. Your participation is voluntary and is based on your consent.

Your personal information is administered in accordance with the federal Privacy Act. As such, you have the right to request access to your personal information held by and the right to ensure that the information is safeguarded and retained according to the retention periods set out in the federal Privacy Act. Furthermore, you have the right to ensure that the information is accurate and up-to-date. Please contact Correctional Service Canada’s Access to Information and Privacy Division at GEN-NHQATIP-AIPRP@CSC-SCC.GC.CA with any questions or concerns you may have.

Your personal information will be retained in Personal Information Bank. Instructions for obtaining this information are outlined in the government publication InfoSource which is available online on CSC’s website at: http://www.csc-scc.gc.ca/info-source/index-eng.shtml
